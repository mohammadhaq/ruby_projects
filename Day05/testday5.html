BASKETBALL: What Is It and How Do You Play

Basketball

Basketball is a team sport. Two teams of five players each try to score by shooting a ball through a hoop elevated 10 feet above the ground. The game is played on a rectangular floor called the court, and there is a hoop at each end. The team with the ball is called the offense. The team without the ball is called the defense.
